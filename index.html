Superman

Superman was born on the fictional planet Krypton and was named Kal-El. As a baby, his parents sent him to Earth in a small spaceship moments before Krypton was destroyed in a natural cataclysm. His ship landed in the American countryside, near the fictional town of Smallville1. He was found and adopted by farmers Jonathan and Martha Kent, who named him Clark Kent
